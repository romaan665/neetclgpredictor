Feedback

Feedback from students and parents helps improve NEET College Predictor. Share what worked well, what was confusing, or any incorrect data you noticed.

Send feedback by email

Write a short message describing your experience, device (mobile/desktop) and, if possible, your NEET rank range.

Feedback email: neetpredictor.feedback@example.com

Fill a short feedback form

It takes less than 2 minutes and helps analyse how useful the predictions are for different rank ranges.

Open feedback form

What to mention in feedback

  • How accurate were the predicted colleges for your rank and category.
  • Any quota / college / course you could not find in the options.
  • Design or usability issues (slow loading, confusing text, mobile layout problems).
Your feedback is used only to improve this tool and will not be shared publicly. For official NEET counselling queries, always contact NTA, MCC or the respective state counselling authority.
