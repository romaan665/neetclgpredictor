How to use NEET College Predictor

Use these steps to enter your details correctly and read the predictions in a useful way during counselling.

1

Create or log in to your account

  • Click Sign up to register with your name, email and password, or use Login if you already have an account.
  • After logging in you will be redirected to the Home page with the prediction form.
2

Enter your NEET details

  • On the Home page, find the form titled NEET UG College Predictor.
  • Type your exact All India Rank (AIR) from the NEET scorecard.
  • Select the correct Category (EWS, General, SC, ST, etc.).
  • Choose the appropriate Quota (All India, Deemed, NRI, etc.) from the dropdown.
  • Select your counselling State and preferred Course (MBBS, BDS, Nursing).
3

Generate predictions

  • Click the Predict My Colleges button.
  • The system will process your input and fetch predictions based on previous years’ closing ranks.
  • Colleges will appear grouped into four sections on the results area of the Home page.
4

Understand the results

  • Round 1 – Guaranteed: Very strong chance of a seat in the first round based on past data.
  • Round 2 – Possible: Good chance if cut‑offs move slightly or if some seats remain after Round 1.
  • Round 3 – Maybe: Borderline options that depend on heavy rank movement or resignations.
  • No Chance: Colleges where your rank has historically been far above the closing rank.

Tips for best use

  • Double‑check your AIR, category and quota before generating predictions.
  • Use the “Guaranteed” and “Possible” lists for realistic choice filling; keep a few “Maybe” options as dream colleges.
  • Re‑run the predictor if your category, quota or state changes between different counselling rounds.
  • Treat the tool as guidance based on historical data, and always confirm final decisions from official counselling notifications.
