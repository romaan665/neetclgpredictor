About NEET College Predictor

Rank-based counselling helper

NEET College Predictor is designed to make counselling planning simpler for MBBS, BDS and Nursing aspirants. By combining your All India Rank with your category, quota, state and course preference, it gives a structured view of colleges that are realistically within reach.

Instead of manually checking multiple PDF cut‑off lists and counselling notices, you get a focused, round‑wise list of colleges so you can spend more time planning and less time searching.

What this tool does

  • Shows colleges where admission is highly likely in Round 1 based on past closing ranks.
  • Highlights colleges that may open up in Round 2 and Round 3 so you can plan backups.
  • Marks colleges that are very difficult to get with your current rank, helping you avoid unrealistic choices.

How it works

  • Uses last 5 years of official NEET counselling closing ranks for multiple rounds and quotas.
  • Applies rank‑based rules and patterns to estimate your chances for different college–course combinations.
  • Groups results into “High chance”, “Possible later rounds” and “Very unlikely” for quick understanding.

Important note

NEET College Predictor is an assistance tool based on historical data, not an official counselling platform. Cut‑offs and rules change every year, so always confirm details from NMC, MCC, state counselling authorities and official college websites before taking final decisions.
